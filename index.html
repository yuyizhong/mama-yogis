Pregnancy & Postnatal Yoga class

Peaceful mind, Easier labour, Quicker recovery and Happier motherhood!

Either you've started the Yoga journey or you are still thinking, I am happy you find us here. We offer a wide range of Yoga classes for mums and mums to be!

Pregnancy Yoga

Reduce stress and anxiety. Increase the strength, flexibility and endurance of muscles needed for childbirth. Decrease lower back pain, nausea, headaches and shortness of breath.

Postnatal Yoga

Increase energy level for busy motherhood. Focus on back strengthening, abdominal toning, pelvic floor recovering and restorative poses.

Mummy and Me

Come to join us to practice Yoga with the little ones! Child-friendly yoga for mothers with kids. Great family activity for moms.

Pre-recorded Online class

Abundance range of yoga styles pre-recorded to suit your needs. Anywhere! Anytime! Signup today for instant access!
